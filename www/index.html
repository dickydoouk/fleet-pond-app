Information

Welcome to the Fleet Pond companion app. You can use this app to plan a walk around the pond, or explore points of interest when you visit. Use the Map tab to view an interactive map of the pond, with trails and points of interested marked. Try the Trails tab to get more information on each trail, including the length of the trail and what points of interest lie along its route. From the Info tab, you can find useful links for the Fleet Pond society website and our social media.

Fleep Pond Society is a charity founded to preserve and maintain Fleet Pond and stimulate public interest and appreciation for the nature reserve. If you would like to donate please click this link:
Donate to Fleet Pond Society

The Hart District Council Countryside Rangers own Fleet Pond and manage the site alongside Fleet Pond Society. To contact the rangers with any queries regarding Fleet Pond, please call +44 1252 623443 or email countryside@hart.gov.uk.
